who we are

come on a learn-to-surf journey of a lifetime

As a mobile surf school we are dedicated to finding the perfect waves daily! Sit back and relax after we pick you up from your accommodation and begin the search for the best, fun, and safest waves in Manu Bay. We have over 10 different locations at our fingertips, ready for the ultimate learn to surf experience. Our beaches are the best in New Zealand where you will learn in waist deep water with easy to ride small waves. We offer programs for complete beginners, intermediate, and advanced surfers of any age.

best surfing by the best surfers

We are the only Manu Bay surf school to have surf school permits to teach personal private lessons & group lessons in both Manu & Whale Bay.

Our surf coaches all:

  • Have current Surfing New Zealand coaching accreditation
  • Have APOLA Beach lifeguard certification
  • A min. 10 years coaching background
  • Have 15 years personal surfing experience in New Zealand and overseas
  • A vast knowledge of the local area
  • Only conduct in surf conditions in waist deep water w/ easy spilling broken waves
